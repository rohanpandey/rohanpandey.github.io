Rohan Pandey

I'm a Product Owner (Engineer 2) at McKinsey & Company working on developing next generation AI-powered spend analytics in digital procurement. Additionly, I have recently conducted research with collaborators at Shiv Nadar University and Healthcare Innovations and Translational Informatics (HITI) Lab at Emory University . Prior to that, I conducted research in TavLab at Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology Delhi. In 2021, I graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science & Engineering from Shiv Nadar University, India with High Distinction.

I am broadly interested in conducting research to make machine learning systems more robust and reliable so that they can work in the wild. I am interested in the many societal applications of such systems – with demonstrated experience in ML for healthcare.

I'm always happy to mentor young professionals starting out their academic or professional journey's. If you'd like to collaborate, drop me an email! Why do I do it? Because people gave me a shot and it changed my trajectory. I am paying it forward.
